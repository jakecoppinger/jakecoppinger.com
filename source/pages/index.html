Jake Coppinger

I'm studying my third year of Computer Science at UNSW in Sydney, though I also love filmmaking, electronics and design.

So far I have represented Australia at the Intel International Science Fair in Los Angeles, presented a talk at TEDxSydney on my gesture recognition research and grown my filmmaking YouTube channel to a profitable 3.6m views/57k subscribers. I also play the violin - solo and with an orchestra.

I've been constantly tinkering since I was handed-down a very old Meccano set at a young age. I attended a Steiner school until year 9, learning various programming languages and electronics in my own time. I then moved to a selective public school so I could pursue science and technology in more depth.

I moved to Sydney to study my passion, and in my spare time I have been visualising realtime Sydney public transit data, using computer vision to visualise how pedestrians use public spaces and exploring notifications as an interface.

I’m a third year computer science student with a passion for code, mechatronics, film, and design.

jake@jakecoppinger.com / based in Sydney
